Commands

In order to use a command, the prefix d! must be used. For example, d!cmd-list will list all the avaliable commands. Depending on the command, further input instructions may be required.

Miscellaneous Some beginner commands, it only gets better from here.

Sends command list - d!cmd-list
Says How can I help you?, maybe - d!hello

Clear Chat Do not let your space be crowed with 'noise', clear it, and begin again.

Delete chat message(s)  - d!msg msg_num

Quotes System Write your story into the book of legends, let Dank Bot be your stage.
